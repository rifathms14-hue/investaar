## Tone Guidelines

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Headlines | Calm, factual |
| CTAs | Confident, action-oriented |
| Legal copy | Formal, precise |
| Market signals | Whisper, observational |
| Success | Confident, not celebratory |
| Errors | Neutral, helpful |
